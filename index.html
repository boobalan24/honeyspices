Black Pepper

Our black peppers are harvesting from Kerala state in south India which is known as native place of black pepper. We are producing one of the finest peppercorns in the world. We are cultivating in organic standards. Ripe fruits with a full core which are picked up by hands, once they turned red. The collected fruits are carefully immersed in boiling water and dried in direct sunlight. After peppercorns are dried, quality of bold pepper is selected with uniform dark colour by experienced hands to getting more consistent flavour and aroma. We are delivering the export quality of peppers in all states of India to bring to you the true flavour of legacy.
